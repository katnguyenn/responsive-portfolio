About Me

My name is Katherine and I am from Los Angeles, California. I have been in marketing for over four years and have decided to transition into the tech industry. This decision came when I started my own online business called Collective Vida, offering branding and website design. I quickly realized that utilizing the drag and drop function on most website building platforms was not enough to bring my vision to the screen, so I learned to code HTML and CSS to help with this issue. Surprisingly, I found it very enjoyable learning these langauges and decided to enroll in a program that would teach me even more.

Currently, I am a student at UCLA Extension Full Stack Development Bootcamp learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, React and more. I am super excited to continue developing my skills and my goal is to work at a clean energy company as their software engineer.
